# CareerCompass 5-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:30 - Problem and Value

CareerCompass helps students turn a resume, coursework, target role, and hiring timeline into a practical career strategy. The demo user is a recent MIS graduate targeting Business Analyst or Project Manager roles in the Bay Area.

## 0:30-1:10 - Agent Workflow

Point to the sidebar workflow. The supervisor coordinates specialized agents for market demand, gap analysis, curriculum planning, resume optimization, interview simulation, and final synthesis. The goal is not one generic answer; it is a structured career plan assembled from specialist outputs.

## 1:10-1:45 - Profile Setup

Show the default resume, target role, location, timeline, and coursework. Explain that the reset button restores the exact sample profile for repeatable demos and recordings.

## 1:45-2:35 - Dashboard

Click **Run CareerCompass analysis**. On the dashboard, highlight readiness, priority gaps, resume keyword coverage, and interview readiness. Then show recommended next moves and one skill gap deep dive.

## 2:35-3:15 - Roadmap

Open **2. Roadmap**. Explain how CareerCompass translates gaps into 30/60/90-day learning actions, including resources, practice tasks, and portfolio proof.

## 3:15-4:00 - Resume Optimization

Open **3. Resume**. Show keyword targets, a before-and-after bullet, the editable draft, and TXT/DOCX export. Emphasize that the system preserves student evidence instead of inventing credentials.

## 4:00-4:35 - Interview Simulation

Open **4. Interview**. Generate scenario questions, choose one, and evaluate a brief answer. Use the score and feedback to show interview practice support.

## 4:35-5:00 - Final Report and Close

Open **5. Final Report** and briefly show the copyable report. End on the human advisor disclaimer and the action checklist: CareerCompass is decision support, not a guarantee.
